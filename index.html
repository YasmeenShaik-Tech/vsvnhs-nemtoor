Who We Are

About Us

Vishwashanthi Vidyaniketan High School, established in 1997, proudly steps into its 30th year of dedicated service in the field of education. Over the years, the school has grown into a trusted institution known for nurturing young minds with knowledge, values, and confidence. We offer quality education from Nursery to Class X in the English medium, following a well-structured academic curriculum supported by experienced and committed teachers. Our focus is not only on academic excellence but also on the overall development of every child. The school is equipped with smart classrooms that make learning interactive and engaging. To prepare students for future challenges, we provide specialized training in IIT-JEE foundation, Olympiads, Robotics, and Artificial Intelligence (AI). Alongside academics, we strongly encourage creativity through Art and Craft activities.
